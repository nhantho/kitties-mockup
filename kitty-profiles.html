Meet our wonderful kitties!

All of our kitties have the perfect amount of flooffness and are ready to purrr right into your heart!

Milo

Milo is always ready to play! His favorite toys are feathers attached to string. He is a true acrobat when it comes to playing, and is sure to bring you hours of entertainment!
